Oh My Dog

Oh My Dog was established in 2010. The business provides high quality grooming at affordable prices by City & Guilds qualified groomers for all breeds of dog.

A pick up and drop off service can be provided within the local area, at a small additional charge, for those who find it difficult to bring their dog to be groomed themselves.

We understand that your dog is part of your family. At Oh My Dog each dog is treated as an individual and we aim to ensure their groom is a fun and pleasurable experience. Each dog is groomed either to breed standard, or to the ownerâ€™s requirements. We use high quality shampoos and conditioners however, if you have your own shampoo we are happy to use this for you.

We provide a range of services ranging from a simple nail trim, to a bath and blow dry, or a full groom. Hand stripping is also available where it is appropriate.

A full groom will include nails clipped, ears cleaned (and plucked where appropriate), bath and style. We will advise you if we spot any potential health issues whilst we are grooming, for example, skin rashes, strange lumps or bumps, or eye or ear infections.
